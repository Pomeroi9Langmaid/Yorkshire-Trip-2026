Itinerary

Seven simple stages, with enough room to adapt the pace.

1 · Mon

Fly to Manchester, drive to York

Land, collect the car and settle into York.

2 · Tue

York

Walkable old streets, the Shambles and a relaxed first full day.

3 · Wed

Helmsley & Cropton

Market-town charm before the Moors.

4 · Wed

North York Moors

Open roads, heather and big countryside views.

5 · Thu

Robin Hood’s Bay

Cobbled lanes, sea views and a proper coastal stop.

6 · Fri

Whitby

Harbour, abbey views and fish and chips.

7 · Sat–Sun

Yorkshire Dales & home

Dales scenery, then an early drive back to Manchester.
