Itinerary

A cleaner seven-part route that still leaves room to adapt each day.

1 · 10 Aug

Fly to Manchester, drive to York

Land, collect the car and settle into York.

2 · 11 Aug

York

Walkable old streets, the Shambles and an easy first full day.

3 · 12 Aug

Helmsley & Cropton

Market-town charm before the Moors.

4 · 12 Aug

North York Moors

Open roads, heather and big countryside views.

6 · 14 Aug

Whitby

Harbour, abbey views and fish and chips.

7 · 15–16 Aug

Yorkshire Dales & home

Dales scenery, then an early run back to Manchester.
